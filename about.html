Project Overview

The Envelope Reader project is made to test tolerances of a variety of different sizes of envelopes. With Cenveo's largest commercial envelope being a number 14 envelope, 4 3/4 in by 11 1/4 in, the Envelope Reader can measure all but 4 of their currently ran envelopes. Once the envelope is placed into the reader employees can click the button on the website to measure the distance between all the different contours drawn with the edge detection we have put in place. Using computer vision the Reader will detect all the edges of the envelope, the outer edge and windows, drawing lines there. Once these lines are drawn the Reader will use an Aruco square to get a baseline measurement and measure the distance between all the different lines. After getting all the measurements the program will insert them into a data table for future reference and tell the employee the distances needed for quality assurance.
